About Us

Our mission is to bring delicious cultural Korean food made at UofT to your tastebuds.

At UofT, we have a diverse population of students. Our Korean population make up large portion of international students.

We provide a diverse flavor bursting package of food. The choices vary from fresh seafood options to vegetarian authenticated options.

This journey began in the creative mind of our creators at the UofT Korean students' association. Established in 2022, we have been open during fall and winter semesters.
